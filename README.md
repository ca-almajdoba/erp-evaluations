# erp-evaluations
Designe and develop an interactive web-based ERP Evaluation Tool that helps organizations assess their business requirements and identify the most suitable ERP solution. 

The tool guides users through a structured evaluation process, covering key functional areas such as Governance & Controls, Finance & Accounting, Operations (Sales/Purchase/Inventory), Technology & Security, People & Adoption .
